
# Superstore Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

The **Superstore Analytics Dashboard** is a data analysis and visualization project built using Power BI to analyze retail sales performance, profitability, and customer insights. The project transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights that help understand business performance across regions, product categories, and customer segments.

This dashboard enables users to explore sales trends, identify top-performing customers and products, and evaluate profitability across different sub-categories.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI – Data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard visualization
* Data Cleaning – Data preparation and transformation within Power BI

## Data Preparation

Before building the dashboard, the dataset was cleaned to improve data quality and accuracy.

Data cleaning steps included:

* Changing incorrect data types
* Replacing inconsistent or incorrect values
* Removing unnecessary columns
* Ensuring numerical fields such as sales, profit, and quantity were correctly formatted

These steps ensured reliable analysis and visualization.

## Dashboard Structure

### Page 1 – Sales Overview

This page focuses on high-level business performance and sales trends.

**Filters**

* Region slicer
* Year slicer

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin
* Average Orders
* Total Quantity

**Visualizations**

* Sales and Profit Line Chart to analyze trends over time
* Sales by Category Bar Chart to compare product category performance
* Sales by State Map to analyze geographic sales distribution

This page helps users quickly understand overall sales performance and regional trends.<br>

### Page 2 – Customer & Product Insights

This page provides deeper analysis of customers and product profitability.

**Visualizations**

* Scatter Plot: Profit vs Quantity to analyze the relationship between sales volume and profitability
* Bar Chart: Top 10 Customers based on total sales
* Donut Chart: Segment Contribution to analyze sales by customer segment
* Combo Chart: Profit and Profit Margin by Sub-category to identify profitable and loss-making products

This page helps identify high-value customers, profitable product categories, and underperforming sub-categories.
